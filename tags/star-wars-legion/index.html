The Tragedy of Darth Vader

A little known fact is that Darth Vader, the primary antagonist in the original Star Wars trilogy (and a primary protagonist in the prequel trilogy) has been referred to by no other than George Lucas as the center-point of the first six films - as “the tragedy of Darth Vader”.

In Star Wars Legion, Fantasy Flight Games most successful foray into a tabletop miniatures game, Vader was the first commander created for the Galactic Empire faction, and one of the most iconic looking units in the game:

Unfortunately, the terrifying looking Dark Lord just doesn’t deliver in the game and this blog will go more into why Darth Vader (as of time of this writing, October 2020) falls short of a competitive piece.
